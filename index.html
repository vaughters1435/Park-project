Welcome to Humbodlt Park

Today, Humboldt Park remains a neighborhood with a strong cultural identity with ongoing activism and community engagement.

The Field House

In 1928, the West Park Commission contructed a fieldhouse in Humboldt Park. The structure was designed by architects Michaelsen and Rognstad.

Flower Field

This ornamental garden is maintained by volunteers from the community for the beautification of the park and enjoyment of park patrons.
